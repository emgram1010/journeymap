Description	Customer places order for washer online or in-store. Order enters OMS with delivery address, product SKU, and preferred delivery window.	OMS confirms product availability, allocates inventory to the order, and verifies special handling requirements (large appliance, White Glove service).	Route optimization software groups stops by zone. Washer delivery assigned to a heavy-appliance capable truck with liftgate.	2-person crew assigned due to large appliance handling requirements. Crew reviews delivery manifest and special instructions (stairs, tight doorways).	WMS directs forklift operator to pick washer from bulk storage. Unit inspected for damage, wrapped in protective blankets, and staged at dock door.	Crew loads washer using pallet jack and straps unit securely. Driver performs truck inspection, confirms manifest, and departs DC.	Truck follows optimized route. GPS tracking active. Driver navigates to delivery stop accounting for traffic and access restrictions.	Driver is 30 min from customer. System triggers ETA notification. Customer confirms someone is home and access is ready.	Crew arrives, introduces themselves, confirms delivery location inside home. Carries washer to designated room using dollies and furniture sliders.	Crew connects water supply hoses, drain hose, and power cord. Runs test cycle to confirm no leaks. Levels machine on floor.	Customer signs digital POD on driver tablet. Photos of installed unit captured. Old appliance hauled away if removal service purchased.	Delivery event closes in OMS. Survey sent to customer. Any damage claims or issues flagged to customer service team.
